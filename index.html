Durgesh Nandini - Kathak & Bharatanatyam Dancer

About

Born on 21st November 1988, Durgesh Nandini is a renowned Kathak and Bharatanatyam dancer. She has trained under esteemed dance gurus and has been performing for over 12 years in Kathak and more than 15 years in Bharatanatyam. She is also the Principal of her own Music and Dance Institute where she imparts training in Kathak, Bharatanatyam, and Western Dance.
